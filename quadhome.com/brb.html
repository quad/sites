Be Right Back

Hi,

I'm healthy, safe, and taking care of myself!

You've received this letter because you may be wondering why I've disappeared. I feel lucky for our mutual experiences unto this point; but, I need to focus on my priorities.

It's my truest hope that we can resume our relationship in the future.

Until then, all my best,

Scott Robinson

Postscript: How to contact

There are two ways to contact me, with two separate rules of engagement:

The first is via text message or email. I read these; but, I won't reply unless the topic is either relevant to my current focus or a life-and-death emergency.

The second is via voice or video call. Calls are scheduled via text message or email and must follow a prearranged agenda.

後記：如何聯絡

有兩種聯絡方式，各有各的規則：

第一個是通過訊息或電子郵件。 我都會讀， 但是，除非話題與我目標 或 生死攸關情況相關， 否則 我不會回答。

第二個是通過語音或視訊。 通過訊息或電子郵件安排通話， 並且必須遵循預先安排的議程。
